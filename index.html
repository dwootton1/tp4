Doug's Guacamole

Ingredients

  • 3 Large Avocados
  • 2 tbsp Cumin
  • 1 tbsp Salt
  • 1/2 cup Chopped Red Onion
  • 1 Lime's worth of juice
  • 1 Jalapeno seeded and veined
  • 3 cloves minced garlic

Kitchen Equipment

  • Chef's knife
  • 1 large mixing bowl
  • Measuring spoons
  • Fork for mixing, something better if you're fancy

Directions

  1. Slice 3 or more large avocados and place in a large mixing bowl
  2. Mince half of a red onion and add it to the large mixing bowl with the avocados
  3. Mince or crush 3 cloves of garlic and add it to the mixing bowl
  4. Seed and vein 1 jalapeno before mincing it and adding it to the mixing bowl
  5. Add 2 tablespoons of Cumin, and 1 tablespoon of salt
  6. Slice a lime in half, juice and add to the mixing bowl
  7. Mix the bowl until it is mixed into a nice creamy guacamole for dipping or adding to taocos

(Original recipe out of Doug's head.)
